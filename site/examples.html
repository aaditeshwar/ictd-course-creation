Case studies grouped by area, with readings arranged in topic columns. Hover or focus a reading to see its abstract when available. This was assembled by pulling together readings from several key venues for computing applied to development and sustainability, such as ACM COMPASS, ICTD, JCSS, IJCAI and AAAI and KDD tracks for social good, CHI, CSCW, FAccT, and previous iterations of the course. Area and topic mapping was done by using local LLMs, and clustering them into readings was done with helps from Claude.
